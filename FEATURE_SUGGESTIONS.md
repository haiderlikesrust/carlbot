# Feature Suggestions for Carl Gaming Companion

## High Priority Features

### 1. **Advanced Search & Discovery**
- **Global Search**: Search users, posts, communities, games all in one place
- **Hashtag System**: #tags for posts (e.g., #ValorantMeta, #ApexBuilds)
- **Trending Hashtags**: Show what's trending right now
- **Search Filters**: Filter by game, community, date, author, etc.
- **Saved Searches**: Save frequently used search queries

- **Post Drafts**: Save posts as drafts
- **Rich Text Editor**: Formatting (bold, italic, lists, code blocks)
- **Post Templates**: Quick templates for common post types

### 3. **User Engagement & Gamification**
- **Achievement System**: Badges for milestones (First Post, 100 Followers, etc.)
- **Leaderboards**: Top users by posts, likes, followers
- **Streaks**: Daily login/post streaks
- **Points/XP System**: Earn points for activity
- **User Levels**: Level up based on activity
- **Reputation Score**: Community reputation based on quality content

### 4. **Real-time Features**
- **Live Notifications**: WebSocket for instant notifications
- **Online Status**: Show who's online/offline
- **Typing Indicators**: In DMs and comments
- **Live Activity Feed**: See what friends are doing in real-time
- **Live Chat Rooms**: Community chat rooms

### 5. **Content Management**
- **Post Editing**: Edit posts after publishing (with edit history)
- **Post Deletion**: Delete posts with confirmation
- **Content Moderation**: Report inappropriate content
- **User Blocking/Muting**: Block or mute users
- **Content Filters**: Filter NSFW, spoilers, etc.

### 6. **Gaming-Specific Features**
- **Match History Tracker**: Track your game matches
- **Build Comparison Tool**: Compare two builds side-by-side
- **Meta Analysis Dashboard**: Visual charts of meta trends
- **Tournament System**: Create and join tournaments
- **Team/Clan System**: Form teams, manage rosters
- **Game Stats Integration**: Connect with game APIs for real stats
- **Strategy Playbooks**: Share and save strategy guides

### 7. **Communication Enhancements**
- **Group Chats**: Multi-user group conversations
- **Voice Messages**: Send voice clips in DMs
- **Video Calls**: Video chat with other users
- **Screen Sharing**: Share screen during calls
- **Message Reactions**: React to DMs with emojis

### 8. **Analytics & Insights**
- **User Dashboard**: Personal analytics (posts, engagement, growth)
- **Post Analytics**: Views, engagement rate, reach
- **Community Analytics**: Member growth, activity stats
- **Game Meta Trends**: Track what's trending in each game
- **Export Data**: Export your posts, messages, etc.

### 9. **Content Discovery**
- **For You Feed**: AI-powered personalized feed
- **Recommended Users**: Suggested users to follow
- **Recommended Communities**: Discover new communities
- **Similar Posts**: "Posts like this" suggestions
- **Weekly Digest**: Email/digest of top content

### 10. **Advanced Community Features**
- **Community Roles**: Admins, moderators, members
- **Community Rules**: Set and enforce rules
- **Community Events**: Schedule events, tournaments
- **Community Polls**: Community-wide polls
- **Community Wiki**: Collaborative knowledge base
- **Community Badges**: Custom badges for members

### 11. **Media & Rich Content**
- **Image Galleries**: Multiple images per post
- **Video Uploads**: Upload and share videos
- **GIF Support**: Search and share GIFs
- **Code Snippets**: Syntax-highlighted code blocks
- **Embed Support**: Embed YouTube, Twitch, etc.

### 12. **Privacy & Security**
- **Privacy Settings**: Control who sees your posts
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enhanced security
- **Activity Log**: View your account activity
- **Download Your Data**: GDPR-compliant data export
- **Account Deletion**: Permanent account deletion

### 13. **Mobile App Features**
- **Push Notifications**: Mobile push notifications
- **Offline Mode**: View cached content offline
- **Quick Actions**: Widgets for quick posting
- **Camera Integration**: Post directly from camera

### 14. **Integration Features**
- **Discord Bot**: Carlbot as Discord bot
- **Twitch Integration**: Share clips, connect accounts
- **Steam Integration**: Show game library, hours played
- **YouTube Integration**: Share videos, embed content
- **Twitter/X Integration**: Cross-post to Twitter

### 15. **Monetization Features** (Optional)
- **Premium Subscriptions**: Premium features
- **Tip Creators**: Tip content creators
- **Sponsored Posts**: Community-sponsored content
- **Merch Store**: Sell Carl merchandise
